## Project Files

- `workbooks/Montgomery_Fleet_Equipment_Inventory_Part1_Cleaned.xlsx`  
  Cleaned inventory dataset with standardized department names, duplicate/blank-row cleanup, trimmed text values, and readable column widths.

- `workbooks/Montgomery_Fleet_Equipment_Inventory_Part2_Analysis.xlsx`  
  Analysis workbook with formatted table, summary calculations, and pivot tables for equipment counts by department and equipment class.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Data cleaning in Excel
- Removing blank rows, duplicate records, and extra spaces
- Standardizing column headers and department data
- Formatting tabular data for readability
- Creating summary calculations
- Building pivot tables for department and equipment-class analysis

## Key Takeaways

- The workbook summarizes `1,582` total equipment items across `12` departments.
- Transportation has the largest equipment count with `1,221` items, making up about `77%` of the analyzed inventory.
- The next largest departments are Permitting Services with `109`, Sheriffs Office with `85`, and Liquor Control with `56`.
- Transit Bus is the most common equipment class with `379` items, followed by Heavy Duty with `290` and Off Road Vehicle Equipment with `283`.
- Equipment inventory can be summarized clearly by department and equipment class using pivot tables and chart previews.
- Clean headers, trimmed text values, and consistent column widths make the workbook easier to audit and reuse.
